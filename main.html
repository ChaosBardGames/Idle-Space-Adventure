The Deck of the Avon Space Station

You stand in an elevator on the Avon Space Station, on the edge of charted space. You're here to begin your own journey as an explorer of the unknown regions.

You chose Avon as your starting point for several reasons. First and foremost, they have cheap spacecraft for sale; you don't own one yet, but you're going to buy one soon.

Secondly, the tax rate is low, at least compared to the core regions. At only 25%, it's almost a third of the taxes on your homeworld.

And of course, Avon is owned by the Zindarian Elite Forces, better known as ZEF. It's a very safe place to start out, as the ZEF regularly patrol the area.

The elevator dings, and you step out onto the market deck. There's a whole selection of surplus ZEF ships that are for sale. You walk over, and the seller wastes no time trying to make a sale.
